## Problem

A boutique personal training studio had the desire to market its premier circuit training studio in the heart of Kensington but lacked the bandwidth to manage its social media presence. **Potential clients in the East Bay generally lacked knowledge of the boutique women-owned gym, opting for national chains with higher brand visibility.**

![A women-owned boutique fitness studio in Kensington grew its client base, expanded to a second location, and built a loyal East Bay community through targeted social media, a redesigned website, and email marketing.](/images/20260609-blog-banners-marina-bay-media-fuse-fitness.png)

## Solution

I worked with the studio to engage high-potential clients via social media and online. I built a modern, responsive website optimized with content to attract upwardly mobile fitness enthusiasts in the East Bay Area. I also nurtured and grew the gym’s caring, supportive community with regular online posts, email newsletters, and online reviews management. 

## Follow-Up

**Inbound leads and referrals via social media continue to pour in for the fitness studio. It has recently expanded to a new location in Albany and has added more personal trainers to its staff to expand its offerings.**
