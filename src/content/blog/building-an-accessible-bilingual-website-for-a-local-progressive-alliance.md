## Solution

I led a full-stack web development project to elevate a local progressive alliance's online presence to better connect with the community. I implemented a modern, user-friendly design with streamlined navigation, ensuring the website effectively showcased the alliance's mission, initiatives, and resources.

To enhance accessibility, I optimized the site for SEO and integrated ADA-compliant features, along with a Spanish language option that broadened our reach. This included translating Spanish-language attachments specifically for pre-election voter education, making critical information accessible to a wider audience during an important election period.

I collaborated closely with the alliance's leadership to align the website's content with our messaging and objectives, handling both front-end and back-end development to support secure hosting, data protection, and smooth functionality. I also set up performance monitoring and leveraged analytics to understand engagement patterns, which helped the alliance refine outreach strategies based on community interaction. Through these efforts, I significantly strengthened the alliance's digital presence, making it easier to connect with constituents and amplifying our impact on local voter education and community engagement.
