## Solution

I led a full-scale digital branding project to elevate the online presence of singer Maddy Hudson, an American Idol hopeful, by creating a modern, visually striking website that showcased her artistry and career milestones.

![I led a full-scale digital branding project to elevate the online presence of singer Maddy Hudson, an American Idol hopeful, by creating a modern, visually striking website that showcased her artistry and career milestones.](/images/20260604-blog-banners-marina-bay-media-maddy-hudson.png)

Working closely with Maddy, I crafted a content strategy that highlighted her powerful vocal talent, featured upcoming performances, and connected fans to her story. I handled both front-end and back-end development, ensuring her site delivered a seamless and professional experience while reflecting her authentic, bold style.

To strengthen her reach, I integrated social media promotion and analytics tools, allowing us to understand audience engagement and optimize content for maximum impact. This helped position Maddy as a rising talent with a polished online presence that resonated with both local fans and industry professionals.
